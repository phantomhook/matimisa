Our Matriarch

A Legacy of Purpose

Matilde’s life touched generations. Her spirit of leadership and radical kindness remains our guiding light.

The Heart of Palomas

Known affectionately as "Tillie," she was the eldest of 11 children. Her early life was defined by responsibility and a deep commitment to family and community.

Vision & Industry

An entrepreneur at heart, she founded grocery stores, pharmacies, and even designed fashion for export, always leading with discipline and creativity.

Radical Kindness

She opened her own classrooms and ensured children were fed. She taught us that true leadership means stepping in where others see obstacles.

A Living Legacy

Matilde leaves behind a vast family including 11 grandchildren and 30 great-grandchildren. Her spirit of "enlightened community" lives on in each of them.
